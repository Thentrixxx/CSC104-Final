One Piece

Episodes: 1116+

Genres: Action, Adventure, Comedy, Drama, Fantasy

Start Date: Oct. 20th, 1999

One Piece

One piece is a pirate anime full of fun adventures! Join Monkey D. Luffy, an eccentric man with rubber powers, as he gathers his crew along his journey to find the "One Piece", the world's greatest treasure, left behind by the world's greatest pirate.

Roronoa Zoro

Once a Pirate Hunter, Roronoa Zoro now travels the seas as Luffy's right hand man.

Thoughts

This is one of my favorite anime, not just for the characters, but also for the world building. Each island has its own personality, and even though the show is pretty long, it was an enjoyable watch all the way through. If you're intimidated by the length, just give it a try. You won't regret it.

10/10





Neon Genesis Evangelion

Episodes: 26

Movies: 5

Genres: Action, Drama, Mecha, Mystery, Psychological, Sci-Fi

Start Date: Oct. 3rd, 1995

Neon Genesis Evangelion

Neon Genesis Evangelion takes place after an alien invasion and multiple planet shattering impacts. Teenagers are required to pilot powerful mechs in order to defeat angelic-esque aliens. At times, this anime can be very down to earth, but at other times, it goes off the rails, embracing Cosmic Horror to the extreme.

Rei Ayanami

Though she seems like an down to earth friend to Shinji at first, she's a part of a lot more than you'd think.

Thoughts

Being among my top 2 anime of all time, this is an insane ride that I would definitely take again. Not only is the plot just genious, the lovecraftian horror of the post-apocalyptic world is just so well done. That being said, the movies take the anime to another extreme, and I can't recommend enough that you watch all of it.

10/10
