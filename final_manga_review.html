Goodnight Punpun

Chapters: 147

Genres: Drama, Psychological, Slice of Life

Start Date: March 15th, 2007

Goodnight Punpun

Goodnight Punpun follows the ordinary life of Punpun Onodera as he grows up from a kid to an adult. However, life isn't so easy, and Punpun learns that the very, very hard way. Reader's discretion is advised.

Punpun Onodera

Punpun Onodera is a kid, teenager, and adult just trying to make his way through life. Unfortunately, life can hit like a freight train.

Thoughts

Though this story is one of the saddest I've ever read, it's also one of the best. Punpun as a character is written so well, and there's fantastic art to accompany it. Don't read this if you're in a bad mood, or if you're in lesser mental health. However, definitely read this if you want to be in the feels for a weekend.

10/10





Dorohedoro

Chapters: 191

Genres: Action, Adventure, Comedy, Fantasy, Horror, Mystery

Start Date: Dec. 30th, 2000

Dorohedoro

A manga where a man named Kaiman, who is part lizard, doesn't know who or what he was. As he teams up with a Gyoza shop owner named Nikaido, he finds out who he was while navigating a dark, yet very goofy world.

Kaiman

Kaiman is a Gyoza loving lizard man who doesn't know his past. Being one of the best and most complex characters written, there's a LOT more to him than you'd think.

Thoughts

This is a manga that I absolutely adore. It was the first manga I ever completed, which lets it hold a special place in my heart. If you want to read a very gore heavy, silly, insane mystery unfold, while having a really fun cast of characters on both the Hero and Villain side, check out this manga.

8.5/10
